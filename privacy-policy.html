Privacy Policy for Phantom Vector

Last updated: July 18, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Asteroids Neon ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play Asteroids Neon. The game functions fully without collecting your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Depending on how you access the game, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically:

  • Game Progress Data: High scores and game settings are stored locally on your device using standard browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device.
  • Device Information: Basic technical data such as screen resolution, browser type, and operating system version may be used to optimize game performance.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous gameplay statistics such as session length and level progression may be collected to improve the game experience.

2.3 Advertising and Analytics

If the version of Asteroids Neon you are playing includes advertisements, third-party advertising partners may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • Coarse location data (city-level only, not precise GPS)
  • Interaction data with advertisements

Our advertising partners may include:

  • Google AdMob (Privacy Policy)
  • Unity Ads (Privacy Policy)

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn off Personalized Ads

3. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely for:

  • Displaying advertisements (to keep the game free)
  • Analyzing game performance and crash reports
  • Improving gameplay experience

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game data (scores, settings) is stored exclusively on your device.
  • No Account Required: Since no account is created, there are no passwords or personal profiles to protect.
  • Data Deletion: Clearing your browser cache or app data will remove all locally stored game information.

5. Children's Privacy

Asteroids Neon is designed to be enjoyable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

In ad-supported versions, we configure ad networks to serve only non-personalized ads to users who have not provided consent for personalized advertising.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (achieved by clearing app data)
  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings
  • GDPR (EU/UK): Right to data portability and restriction of processing
  • CCPA (California): Right to know what data is collected and opt out of sale

7. Third-Party Links

The game may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.
